Ham-Burger

Description

A hamburger is a sandwich consisting of a cooked meat patty on a bun or roll. You can order a hamburger, fries, and a shake at most fast food restaurants. Hamburgers are traditionally made with ground beef and served with onions, tomatoes, lettuce, ketchup, and other garnishes.

Ingredients

  • 1 large egg
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • ½ cup fine dry bread crumbs
  • Burger bun
  • Lettuce
  • Onions
  • Tomato slices

Steps

  1. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil grate.
  2. Whisk egg, salt, and pepper together in a medium bowl.
  3. Add ground beef and bread crumbs; mix with your hands or a fork until well blended.
  4. Form into four 3/4-inch-thick patties.
  5. Place patties on the preheated grill. Cover and cook 6 to 8 minutes per side, or to desired doneness. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 160 degrees F (70 degrees C).
  6. Heat the burger buns for a little crisp
  7. Put the onion slices and tomato slices altogether with the patty along with lettuce
  8. Serve hot and enjoy!
