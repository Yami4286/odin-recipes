Birayni

Description

Simply put, biryani is a spiced mix of meat and rice, traditionally cooked over an open fire in a leather pot. It is combined in different ways with a variety of components to create a number of highly tasty and unique flavor combinations.

Steps

For Marination

  1. In a bowl , take yogurt and add ajwan + garm masala + green chlli chop + ginger-garlic paste + turmeric + salt + crush chilli + red chilli powder + biryani masala + corriander
  2. Then mix it all well in a paste
  3. Add the chicken in paste for marination
  4. Put it in fridge for a while

Biryani Qurma

  1. First, take a Handi and add oil and onion and let it cook until it gets a little golden color
  2. Cut the tomato in slices ad then add marinated chicken in it
  3. Add potatoes cut in moon shape
  4. Add tomato , then sprinkle black pepper and salt
  5. In a Handi, then boil water and add salt and vinegar
  6. In a bowl, add star anise, cummin, elachi, clove, black pepper , garam masala and then put in boiling water
  7. Add boiled rice and food color mix with water in qourma

Biryani Process

  1. In a Handi, add oil as first layer
  2. Then add potatoes and second layer rice
  3. Add food color
  4. Third layer is qourma
  5. Then add onion and lemon and green chili
  6. fourth layer is rice
  7. Fifth layer is qurma
  8. We add little onion and lemon
  9. Sixth layer is rice and upper sixth layer is we add green chili and lemon and onion
  10. Now cook it at low flame for 20-25 minutes
